Description:
Preclin MRI samples repository provides examples of
Bruker7T native and DICOM samples for mouse leg MRI of multi-echo 
proton-density fat-fraction (PDFF), 2-b-value diffusion weighted imaging (DWI) & derived
apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) maps, and 3D FLASH magentization transfer (MT) "on"
and "off" scans.
